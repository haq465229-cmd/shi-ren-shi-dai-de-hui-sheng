# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Readers, collaborators, and worldbuilding reviewers exploring the TIERIA setting through an atmospheric web archive. They arrive to browse lore, trace eras and regions, and read long-form setting entries without losing the sense of entering a mythic library.

## Product Purpose

The site turns the Word manuscript for "诗人时代的回声" into a navigable, image-rich web experience. Success means the manuscript's hierarchy, prose, and artwork are complete on the site, with clear jumps from volumes to sections to individual entries.

## Brand Personality

Mythic, solemn, archival. The interface should feel like a dark gallery of illuminated books: cinematic on entry, calm and readable once the reader opens a volume.

## Anti-references

Do not make the reading experience feel like a generic documentation site, a card-heavy SaaS landing page, or a flat file directory. Avoid fake controls, decorative clutter, and horizontal chapter layouts that make long Chinese titles hard to scan.

## Design Principles

- Preserve the existing dark, theatrical TIERIA identity.
- Make hierarchy obvious before adding ornament.
- Let the manuscript remain the source of truth.
- Favor deep reading and stable navigation over novelty.
- Keep motion and atmosphere supportive, never obstructive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Support keyboard navigation, readable contrast, reduced motion, descriptive image alt text, and responsive layouts for desktop and mobile reading.
